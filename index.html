Simple Browser

Privacy Policy

Plain language. No legalese.

Effective: March 6, 2025
The short version Simple Browser does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data whatsoever. No accounts. No tracking. No analytics. Your browsing stays entirely on your device.

📦 What We Collect

Nothing. Simple Browser does not collect any personal information, device identifiers, usage statistics, crash reports, or any other data from you or your device.

🔒 Your Browsing Data

All browsing activity — including the websites you visit, your browsing history, cookies, and cached content — is stored locally on your device only using Android's built-in WebView. We have no access to this data and it is never sent to us.

  • Browsing history stays on your device
  • Cookies are managed by Android WebView locally
  • You can clear all data from within the app at any time

🌐 Third-Party Websites

Simple Browser is a tool to access websites on the internet. When you visit a website, that website may collect data according to its own privacy policy. We have no control over and no responsibility for the privacy practices of any third-party websites you visit.

🤝 Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties — because we don't collect any data in the first place. There are no advertisers, analytics providers, or data brokers involved with this app.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes in the future, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date. We encourage you to review it periodically.
